Market Research

Emotional vs. Rational Brand Storytelling

Conducted a market research study (Final Thesis) analyzing how emotional and rational storytelling influence consumer trust and brand perception.

Thesis: Heart vs. Head

Hypothesis: Emotional storytelling builds better long-term loyalty than rational spec-sheets.

Methodology: A/B testing two campaigns for a fictional tech product on 500+ participants across Germany and China to see cultural variances.

Result: Emotional ads had 40% higher recall. However, rational data was required to "close" the deal for high-ticket items.

Conclusion: Use emotion to attract, use logic to justify.
